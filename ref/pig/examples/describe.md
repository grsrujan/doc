We can use the DESCRIBE and ILLUSTRATE operators to view the schema.

DESCRIBE A;</br>
a: {f1: int,f2: int,f3: int}
</br>

To view the step-by-step execution of a series of statements.

ILLUSTRATE A;</br>
---------------------------------------------------------
| a     | f1: bytearray | f2: bytearray | f3: bytearray |
---------------------------------------------------------
|       | 4             | 2             | 1             |
---------------------------------------------------------
</br>
---------------------------------------
| a     | f1: int | f2: int | f3: int |
---------------------------------------
|       | 4       | 2       | 1       |
---------------------------------------
